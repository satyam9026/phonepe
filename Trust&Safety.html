Security Tips for Users

Protect your PhonePe account and transactions with these essential security practices.

Secure Your PIN

Never share your UPI PIN with anyone. PhonePe will never ask for your PIN via call, message, or email.

Learn More

Device Security

Keep your phone locked with a strong password or biometric authentication. Install security updates regularly.

Device Safety

Network Safety

Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions. Use your mobile data or a secure private network.

Network Tips

Recognizing Phishing Attempts

Suspicious Emails

PhonePe will never send you emails asking for personal information or login credentials. Report suspicious emails.

Report Email

Fake Messages

Beware of SMS or WhatsApp messages claiming to be from PhonePe with links asking for personal details.

Report Message

Fake Calls

PhonePe representatives will never call you asking for OTP, PIN, or bank account details. Hang up immediately.

Report Call

Safety Guidelines for Users

Do's

  • Set a strong and unique UPI PIN
  • Enable biometric authentication
  • Verify recipient details before payment
  • Keep your PhonePe app updated
  • Use official PhonePe channels for support
  • Check transaction history regularly
  • Log out when using shared devices

Don'ts

  • Never share your UPI PIN with anyone
  • Don't click on suspicious links
  • Avoid public Wi-Fi for transactions
  • Don't share OTP with anyone
  • Avoid saving UPI PIN on your device
  • Don't trust calls asking for personal details
  • Avoid downloading apps from unknown sources
