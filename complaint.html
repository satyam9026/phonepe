Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about the complaint process

How long does it take to resolve a complaint?

Most complaints are resolved within 7-10 business days. However, complex issues may take longer. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

What information do I need to file a complaint?

Please have the following ready: Your registered phone number/email, transaction ID (if applicable), details of the issue, and any supporting documents like screenshots.

Can I track the status of my complaint?

Yes, once you file a complaint, you'll receive a unique complaint ID. You can use this ID to track the status on this page or through the PhonePe app.

What happens after I submit a complaint?

You'll receive an acknowledgment with your complaint ID. Our team will review your case, investigate, and contact you for any additional information. You'll be notified once resolved.

What should I do if I suspect fraudulent activity?

Immediately file a complaint and select "Suspected Fraud" as the complaint type. Also, contact your bank to secure your account and change your PhonePe PIN.
